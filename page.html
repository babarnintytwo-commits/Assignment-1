    EnglishThe **Holy Quran** is the final and most sacred book revealed by **Allah (SWT)** to His last Prophet, **Muhammad (peace be upon him)**, as a complete guidance for all of humanity. It was revealed over a period of 23 years through the Angel **Jibreel (Gabriel)** in the Arabic language. The Quran is a divine miracle, unmatched in its eloquence, wisdom, and truth. It contains the words of Allah Himself, providing guidance on every aspect of life — faith, worship, morality, justice, and social conduct. The Quran begins with the beautiful chapter **Surah Al-Fatiha** and ends with **Surah An-Naas**, reminding believers of the importance of mercy, purity, and devotion to their Creator. In Islam, the Quran is not just a book to be read, but a **complete way of life**. It teaches Muslims to live with righteousness, kindness, and patience, and to always seek closeness to Allah. Muslims recite verses of the Quran in their daily prayers and strive to follow its teachings in every action. The Quran emphasizes unity, peace, forgiveness, and the remembrance of Allah. It is a source of light and healing for hearts, and its message remains eternal and unchanged — guiding people from darkness into the light of truth and faith.
